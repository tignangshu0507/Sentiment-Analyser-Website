MODELING AND PREDICTION

Our model is trained using the Multinomial Naive Bayes Classifier, which is a specific instance of a Naive Bayes classifier which uses a multinomial distribution for each of the features. The f1 score of the model is 0.927 (92% accuracy). Thus finally we are predictig the tweet sentiment with our model and showing the user what does the market regard him as, whether his tweets are positive one or they do suffer an adverse effect.
